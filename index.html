Capabilities

Every tool to bridge
you and the art.

Five integrated detection systems work together so you never miss a piece, whether behind glass or across the street.

AI Visual Recognition

CLIP ViT-B/32 runs entirely on your phone via ONNX WASM — no server round-trips, no images leaving your device. The model computes 512-dimensional visual embeddings from the live camera feed and matches against our indexed artwork database in real time.

GPS Proximity

Always-on position tracking with haversine distance calculates your proximity to outdoor artworks. Walk within range and a notification fires automatically.

Tracking active

QR Scanning

jsQR reads camera frames every 300ms. Point at a printed Gestalt label and the artwork loads instantly.

AI-Generated Audio Guides

Gemini 2.5 Flash writes rich, museum-quality descriptions from the artwork image alone. Google Neural2-F TTS delivers them as natural speech — with haptic confirmation so blind and low-vision visitors never need to look at the screen.

Multi-Venue

Museums, galleries, public spaces, and street art all share one unified platform with separate location management, floor plans, and accessibility metadata.

ADA Compliant

Built for the DOJ's 2024 digital accessibility mandate. Zero infrastructure cost per venue. Haptic feedback, audio-first design, and automatic compliance scoring help institutions meet enforceable timelines.

Three Steps

Point. Discover. Listen.

No accounts, no downloads required for the web version. Just open the link and your camera does the rest.

01

Point your camera

Open Gestalt on your phone. The camera activates, CLIP loads on-device, and GPS begins tracking your position relative to nearby outdoor artworks. No app download required for the web version.

02

Discover the artwork

Tap "Scan" and CLIP matches your camera frame against our artwork index in under a second. No QR code needed, no beacon, no staff intervention. Outdoors, simply walk closer and GPS handles it.

03

Listen to the story

The artwork info card slides up with the title, artist, year, and an AI-generated description. Audio fires immediately with haptic confirmation — the experience works for blind visitors without ever looking at the screen.
