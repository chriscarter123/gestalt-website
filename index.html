Capabilities

Every tool to bridge
you and the art.

Five integrated detection systems work together so you never miss a piece, whether behind glass or across the street.

AI Visual Recognition

MobileNet v1 computes 1024-dimensional embeddings from the live camera feed, then runs cosine similarity matching against indexed artwork images. Two-tier detection with keyword fallback ensures recognition works even while embeddings are still being computed.

GPS Proximity

Always-on position tracking with haversine distance calculates your proximity to outdoor artworks. Walk within range and a notification fires automatically.

Tracking active

QR Scanning

jsQR reads camera frames every 300ms. Point at a printed Gestalt label and the artwork loads instantly.

AI-Generated Audio Guides

Gemini 2.5 Flash writes rich, museum-quality descriptions from the artwork image alone. Google Cloud TTS converts them to natural-sounding neural voice MP3s ready to play in your ear.

Multi-Venue

Museums, galleries, public spaces, and street art all share one unified platform with separate location management, floor plans, and accessibility metadata.

Three Steps

Point. Discover. Listen.

No accounts, no downloads required for the web version. Just open the link and your camera does the rest.

01

Point your camera

Open Gestalt on your phone. The camera activates, MobileNet loads in the background, and GPS begins tracking your position relative to nearby outdoor artworks.

02

Discover the artwork

Tap "Start Scan" and the AI matches your camera frame against indexed artwork embeddings in real time. Indoors, scan a QR label. Outdoors, simply walk closer and GPS handles it.

03

Listen to the story

The artwork info card slides up with the title, artist, year, and a rich AI-generated description. Tap the audio button for a neural-voice guide that brings the piece to life.

On-device intelligence,
cloud-grade content.

Visual recognition runs entirely in the browser using TensorFlow.js. No images ever leave your device for matching. MobileNet extracts 1024-dimensional feature embeddings and compares them via cosine similarity, fast enough for real-time video frames.

When Gestalt finds a match, Gemini 2.5 Flash generates a rich audio-guide-quality description. Google Cloud TTS (Neural2-F voice) converts it to natural speech. Firebase handles storage, authentication, and real-time data sync, with a REST API bypass for smooth performance inside iOS WKWebView.
