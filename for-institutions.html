For Institutions

Accessibility that scales

Writing audio descriptions for an entire collection would take years and cost hundreds of thousands of dollars. Gestalt does it in days. Our AI generates vivid, accurate descriptions at scale — then your team reviews, edits, and publishes through a dashboard built for curators.

Why audio description hasn't happened

2-3%
of museum collections have audio descriptions
$50-150
cost per description when written by hand

It's not that museums don't care about accessibility — they do. It's that the traditional approach doesn't scale. Hiring specialists to write descriptions for 10,000 works, then recording professional voice actors, then maintaining the content as exhibitions change... it's a project that never ends and never finishes.

AI-generated, curator-refined

Upload your collection

Connect your collection database or upload images directly. Gestalt ingests your existing metadata — titles, artists, dates, curatorial notes — and uses it to inform descriptions.

Generate descriptions

Our AI writes rich, vivid audio descriptions for every work. Not placeholder text — real descriptions that capture visual composition, artistic technique, and historical context.

Review and refine

Your curators review descriptions in a purpose-built CMS. Edit text, adjust emphasis, add institutional voice. Approve when ready.

Publish and track

One click deploys descriptions to visitors. Analytics show which works are most listened to, where visitors spend time, and how your accessibility coverage grows.

ADA-ready, grant-ready

Gestalt generates ADA compliance scorecards for your collection — showing exactly what percentage of works have descriptions, which galleries are fully covered, and where gaps remain. Export PDF reports for grant applications, board presentations, and regulatory documentation.

Real-time coverage tracking

Dashboard shows description status for every work: complete, in review, pending, or missing.

Exportable reports

One-click PDF generation for compliance documentation, funder reports, and accessibility audits.

Works on visitors' own phones

Forget beacon installations, device rentals, and charging stations. Gestalt runs on the smartphone visitors already have. No capital expenditure, no maintenance burden, no staff training required.

Street art. Sculpture trails. Anywhere.

Gestalt isn't limited to museums. Public art commissions, outdoor sculpture gardens, mural districts, walking tours — any art that exists in physical space can be indexed and described. We're building the first comprehensive audio layer for public art.
